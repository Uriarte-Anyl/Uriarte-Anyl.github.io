DISCOVER

Discover the untouched beauty of Batanes, a remote paradise in the northern Philippines known for its breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage. From dramatic cliffs and rolling hills to charming stone houses, Batanes offers a journey of discovery unlike any other.

Basco Lighthouse

This iconic structure in Naidi Hills offers sweeping views of Basco, the sea, and surrounding hills, making it a perfect spot for sunrise and sunset.

Marlboro Hills

Famous for its expansive, green rolling hills and scenic views of the Pacific Ocean and Mt. Iraya, often likened to landscapes in New Zealand.

Valugan Boulder Beach

A unique beach covered in smooth, volcanic boulders with dramatic views of waves crashing against the rocks.

Sabtang Island

Known for its well-preserved Ivatan stone houses and stunning natural landscapes, offering a glimpse into traditional Ivatan life.

BREATHTAKING VIEWS

Batanes is a serene destination known for its breathtaking views of dramatic cliffs, rolling hills, and expansive coastlines. With panoramic landscapes at every turn, visitors are captivated by its untouched beauty and peaceful charm.

GO TO DESTINATIONS

Batanes is one of the Philippines' go-to destinations for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty, featuring lush rolling hills, rugged coastlines, and pristine beaches. With its peaceful landscapes and rich cultural heritage, it offers a unique escape for adventure seekers and cultural enthusiasts alike.
